Haram Foods

A guide to ingredients, meat, and hidden derivatives.

Pork & Derivatives

Includes lard, pork fat, and any ingredient derived from swine.

Improper Slaughter

Meat not slaughtered according to Sharia (Zabiha) or dedicated to other than Allah.

Blood & Carnivores

Consuming flowing blood or animals with fangs/talons is prohibited.

🔍 The E-Number Watchlist

Often derived from pork or non-halal animals. Check labels carefully:

E120
E441
E471
E542
E904
E920

Gelatin Safety

Gelatin is found in candies, marshmallows, and yogurts. Look for "Halal Certified" or "Bovine Gelatin" (only if Zabiha).
