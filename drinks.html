Haram Drinks

Understanding the prohibitions and finding pure alternatives.

⚠️ Common Prohibitions

Alcoholic Beverages

Khamr (Intoxicants) are strictly forbidden in any quantity, including wine, beer, and spirits.

Hidden Ethanol

Certain soft drinks or flavorings may use alcohol as a solvent. Always check for "Non-Alcoholic" certification.

Cross-Contamination

Drinks prepared in the same shakers or glasses used for alcohol without proper cleaning (Istihala context).

✨ Pure Alternatives

Mocktails

Fruit-based botanical infusions.

Natural Juices

100% pure squeezed fruits.

Sparkling Water

With natural mint or lemon.

Traditional Teas

Herbal, Green, or Black teas.
