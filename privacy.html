Privacy Policy

How we protect and respect your digital journey.

Data Collection

PurePath is a static information guide. We do not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII) from our users. Your browsing journey remains entirely yours.

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that monitor your behavior across other websites. Any local settings (like language preference) are stored only on your device.

Your Rights

As we do not hold your data, there is no personal information to delete or modify. We believe in complete digital transparency and spiritual integrity.
