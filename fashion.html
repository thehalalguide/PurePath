Modest Fashion

Exploring the ethics of clothing and the beauty of modesty.

The Essence of Haya

Universal Standards

Clothing that is loose, non-transparent, and covers the required areas (Awrah) for both men and women.

Intentional Style

Avoiding clothing designed specifically for vanity, arrogance, or to imitate harmful cultural trends.

Ethical Sourcing

🧵

Fair Trade

Supporting brands that ensure fair wages and safe conditions for workers.

♻️

Sustainability

Choosing quality over quantity to reduce "Fast Fashion" waste.

🐑

Pure Materials

Being mindful of animal-derived products like certain silks or furs.

🤲

Mindful Disposal

Donating or recycling old clothing instead of discarding it.
